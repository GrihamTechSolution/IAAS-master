1. Village Concept Projects

VCPs are large scale development project in rural village with a measurable social impact and a direct contribution to the SDG 2030.Youth and students all over the world organize this project. This projects contribute directly to the Sustainable Development Goal-

1. No poverty

8. Decent work and economic growth

10. Reduced inequalities

There are 19 projects currently documented in our booklets as we are about to see more project coming afterwards the project competition. Currently IAAS Indonesia is most active in Village Concept Projects.

2. Women in Agriculture Project

The Women in Agriculture (WIA) project is an initiative that seeks to address the issue of gender inequality in the agricultural space. This Global Project was officially started on the 8th of March 2018(International Woman's Day). The road to this great initiative towards achieving Sustainable Development Goal 5 started during 59th and 60th World Congress. They meet the core objectives of providing a platform that creates awareness about SDG 5 which is Gender Equality

3. No Food Waste Project

IAAS World launched this No Food Waste Project with the intention of raising awareness of food waste. This project coordinating youths are committed to supporting innovative and novel projects that address the pressing issues of (food) waste. Its aim is to contribute to the Sustainable Development Goal 2,12,13

2. Zero Hunger

12. Responsible Consumption and Production

13. Climate Action

4. IAAS One Health Project

Antibiotic resistance, vector borne illness, food safety and security, mental health, environmental contamination and other issues can all be helped by IAAS One health approach.

One health contributes to SDGs 1,2,3,12,17 ( No Poverty, Zero Hunger, Good Health and Well being, responsible consumption and production, partnerships) and SDGs 5,6,10,15 ( gender equality, water and sanitation, reduced inequality, life on land).

5. IAAS Delta Project

IAAS Delta Project is a global network towards cooperation and collaboration in between public, private and civil efforts for sustainable management of deltas. The aim of this project is to foster developments towards prosperity in deltas worldwide.

IDP directly contributes to SDG 6(Clean Water and Sanitation) and SDGs 2(Zero Hunger),8(Decent Work and Economic Growth),9,14(Life below water).

Project has as its main mission the protection and conservation of deltas all around the world towards knowledge transfers and dissemination, international cooperation and sustainable local management.
