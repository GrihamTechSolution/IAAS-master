The Study Abroad Program is a communication tool provided by IAAS World to your university. Thus, if you want your university to be part of the IAAS network and have full access to the study abroad opportunities from our university’s partners worldwide, check our SAP brochure, our guide on how to promote the SAP and contact us at studyabroad@iaasworld.org.

Why studying abroad?

In today's world, language and intercultural skills are key to success. Discovering new places, immersing yourself in a new culture, meeting people from all over the world are proven factors for long-term success. For us, success isn’t just a possibility; it’s a promise. Studying abroad has challenges and rewards, and we support you in every step of the way so you know your investment will pay off. Besides your time abroad, the learning doesn’t stop when you return home: you’ll stay connected to your study abroad experience through online learning tools and student communities. You won’t find a more reliable study abroad program anywhere else!
