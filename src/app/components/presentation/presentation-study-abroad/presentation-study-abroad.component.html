Why promote study abroad program with us?



We are the biggest student organization in agricultural and related sciences worldwide. We want to give our members an opportunity to access a higher level of study with the universities that we have partnered up with.

What are the benefits of studying abroad?



In today's world, language and intercultural skills are key to success. Discovering new places, immersing yourself in a new culture and making new friends from all over the world are proven factors for long-term success.

For us, progress isn’t just a possibility; it’s a promise. Studying abroad has challenges and rewards, and we support you in every step of the way so you know your investment will pay off. The learning doesn’t stop when you return home: you’ll stay connected to your study abroad program through online learning tools and student communities.

What’s more, you go home with documented proof of your language proficiency if you've studied in another language: an academic portfolio of certifications and exam results that are recognized by employers, universities and governments around the world.

You won’t find a more reliable, more experienced study abroad program anywhere. Other benefits of studying abroad are:

• Learn a new language
• Discover a new culture
• Meet new people from all over the world
• Gain valuable experiences, both personally and professionally
