The IAAS Study Abroad Program encourages students’ part of the IAAS network to study abroad in universities and faculties proposing programs in the fields of agronomy, food sciences, bioscience engineering, environmental sciences, agri-business, economics and politics.

The IAAS Study Abroad Program gives students the chance to find the right study program among a network of trustworthy IAAS universities where the local members provide help for the international students’ integration.

What's more, it gives universities the possibility to reach more than 10.000 students in more than 50 countries in order to extend their visibility and attractivity worldwide.
